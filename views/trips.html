Tunis Village

Tunis Village in Fayoum is a rural village and one of the most beautiful tourist places in Fayoum. It enjoys some tourist attractions, restaurants, and recreational activities such as teaching pottery and ceramics, horse and boat riding on Lake Qarun, and accommodations such as hotels, villas, and private chalets, and its proximity to the waterfalls of Wadi El Rayan and Wadi El Hitan.
